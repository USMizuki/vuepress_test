Bio

I am an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology, at Ocean University of China. I received the Ph.D. degree from South China University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Shengfeng He and Prof. Guoqiang Han in December 2019. I received the B.Sc. degree and M.Eng. degree in computer science and technology from Jiangnan University under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Wu, in 2011 and 2014 respectively.

My research interests include image processing, computer vision, deep learning and representation learning. I am currently looking for brilliant and self-motivated students, to explore intriguing and inventive computer vision and image processing projects. If you are interested, please feel free to send me your CV and transcripts.
