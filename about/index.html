Yong Du
Assistant Professor at Ocean University of China

I am from tsingtao (Shandong), a beautiful coastal city in northern China. In January 2020, I joined OUC as an assistant professor. My research interests include image processing, computer vision, deep learning and representation learning.

We welcome brilliant and self-motivated students to join our team, that is, Lab of Vision intelligence (Lance), to explore intriguing and inventive computer vision and image processing projects. If you are interested, please feel free to send me your CV and transcripts.
